My Culture

I have a mix of both Taiwanese culture and New Zealand culture. In Taiwanese culture, we enjoy eating many
foods and having festivals. Some popular Taiwanese foods include gua bao, sticky rice, and Taiwanese fried chicken. We
also have foods that are specifically eaten on holidays. For example, on Lunar New Year we eat turnip/radish cakes. These
are a personal favourite of mine and we eat it becuase in chinese, cake means "tall" and radish is a homophone for
"good fortune". While we do eat it on Lunar New Year especially, it's still a very popular dish among Taiwanese people all throughout the year.
